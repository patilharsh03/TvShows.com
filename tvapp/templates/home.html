Upcoming Shows

The Mandalorian Season 2

The Mandalorian seems to have made almost everyone Star Wars fan happy. Season 1 of the first ever live-action Star Wars TV succeeded in the difficult task of presenting a show with entirely new characters that still captured the spirit of what fans love about the franchise, as well as introducing the world to the pop culture phenomenon that is Baby Yoda.

The Haunting Of Bly Manor

2018's The Haunting of Hill House was a smashing-success for Netflix,so it wasn't any surprise when it was announced in February this year that Season 2 is happening.The new season is based on the 1898 Henry James novella The Turn of The Screw.

His Dark Materials Season 2

His Dark Materials is HBO's adaptation of Philip Pullman trilogy of fantasy novels. Even though Season 1 only premiered in December last year, Season 2 had nearly finished shooting before the pandemic shut down most of Hollywood in March and will arrive this side of Christmas. This season will adapt the second of Pullman's novels, The Subtle Knife.

WandaVision

WandaVision will be the first MCU show to hit Disney+. We've known for some time that it wouldn't be a standard superhero show, and the recent trailer revealed just how strange it promises to be. Vision (Paul Bettany) and Scarlet Witch (Elizabeth Olsen) are now living in suburbia, trying to maintain "normal" lives, and much of the trailer suggests this will be presented in a classic sitcom format. But as MCU fans know, Vision was killed in Infinity War--something the trailer alludes to--and things are definitely not what they seem.

The Falcon And The Winter Soldier

The Falcon and The Winter Soldier was set to debut in August 2020, but will now arrive in 2021. Anthony Mackie and Sebastian Stan return to play their respective roles, and they'll be joined by Daniel Brühl reprising his role of Zemo from Captain America: Civil War.

Mirzapur Season 2

Amazon Prime Video made the most awaited announcement. Mirzapur Season 2 will premiere on the streaming platform on October 23, 2020. The makers of the web series revealed its release date via a teaser, which hints that Mirzapur 2 is going to be bigger and better.

House of the Dragon

Based on George R.R. Martin’s book Fire & Blood, the series will tell the story of House Targaryen (a.k.a. the House that gave us the Mother of Dragons herself, Daenerys Targaryen) and take place 300 years before the events of Game of Thrones.

Succession Season 2

If you’re not caught up on the Succession finale yet, then don’t worry. It’s very quietly become the best show on TV – so you’re not the only tellyhead to have missed out. Set in big city America, the HBO drama follows billionaire Logan Roy as he pits his children against one another with a view to naming the successor to his business empire.

Better Call Saul Season 6

If you’re not yet watching AMC’s Better Call Saul after five excellent seasons, you’re missing out on arguably television’s top drama. If, somehow, you have yet to watch both Breaking Bad and Better Call Saul, you have no idea what you missed.
